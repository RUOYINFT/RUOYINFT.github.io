About RUOYI

RUOYI is a group of NFTs generated using 3D rendering technology and based on Asian girls, for which we have prepared different combinations of antique, modern and technological styles.

RUOYI is dedicated to providing a charity fund for underprivileged underage women worldwide. We plan to collect 7% of Creator Fees from each transaction and donate half of this to girls who need our help.

MANIFESTO

We are tired of meaningless FOMO and we want to return to the original idea of WEB3, to use the power of the crowd to bring hope to people struggling to make ends meet.

We believe that love is the light at the end of the tunnel,
when arrogance and prejudice have been removed.
